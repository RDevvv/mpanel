    abhishek dadoo
    (Mr. ProveIt)
    Founder, CEO

    Abhishek is a Telecoms & Mobile industry veteran and has worked in Management Consulting, Principal Investments, and Strategy Development. He holds a dual degree in Mathematics & Computer Science from The College of Wooster (USA), MBA from INSEAD, and has worked at PwC (USA), Altman Vilandrie & Co (USA), and Ericsson Telecommunications (Singapore).

    In late 2012 he founded GullakMaster - a mobile marketing platform for brick and mortar stores in India. With mobile becoming the point of first contact for the Indian populous, Abhishek believes that mobile marketing presents a large opportunity for digital marketers. While a lot is being done to direct online traffic to ecommerce portals, Abhishek believes that there is little innovation being done to convert mobile traffic to offline footfall for physical stores; And in the Indian context, physical stores are here to stay for a very long time. Hence, he set out to solve this problem and launched GullakMaster.

    Abhishek welcomes feedback and learns from his mistakes. Surrounded by numbers and data, he is a big proponent of data driven decisions. He will refute any assumption unless it is proven; so much so that the entire GullakMaster team has learnt the same gospel. At the moment, Abhishek is busy becoming a growth hacker and goes by the nickname 'Mr. ProveIt'.

Careers

At GullakMaster we entrust employees with high responsibility and fast-track career growth opportunities. At our office, there are no hierarchies and you are able to execute on your ideas with speed. And the best part – our pay is competitive! Interested candidates please send your resume to careers@gullakmaster.com. Kindly mention the job title in the subject line of your email.
 
JUNIOR UI DEVELOPER
Looking for a UI professional with 1-3 years of experience in the following areas:
  • Having knowledge of basic HTML tags like Doctypes, forms, paragraphs, table and tableless layouts etc.
  • Should be having knowledge of CSS upto CSS 2.0. CSS3.0 knowledge is desirable.
  • HTML4/5, CSS 2.0(version 3 is desirable), Basic understanding of Javascript/jQuery, OOPS concepts, MVC(desired), Any version control systems.
  • Should be able to differentiate various JS libraries like jquery, prototype, moo tools etc.
  • Should have the knowledge of picking any js plugin and customizing it as per requirements.
  • Should be able to design HTML emailers and hence should have hands on experience of design tools like photoshop.
  • Creating image slices, using grids will be used many a times.
  • Should have worked on any file version control systems like SVN, Git etc. Although hands on experience on Git is desired.
  • Fresher’s are welcome.
 
In this role there is an opportunity to convert to a permanent employee depending upon your performance.
 
MARKETING (INTERN)

  • Must be enthusiastic about working in a startup.

  • Fresher’s are welcome.
  • Help to implement SMO Strategy & Developing Brand Awareness.

  • Monitor trends in social media tools, trends and applications.
  • Conducting Brand & Competitors Analysis on Digital Platforms.
  • Engaging people on social media and figuring new ideas to do it.
 
In this role there is an opportunity to convert to a permanent employee depending upon your performance.
