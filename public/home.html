    abhishek dadoo
    (Mr. ProveIt)
    Founder, CEO

    Abhishek is a Telecoms & Mobile industry veteran and has worked in Management Consulting, Principal Investments, and Strategy Development. He holds a dual degree in Mathematics & Computer Science from The College of Wooster (USA), MBA from INSEAD, and has worked at PwC (USA), Altman Vilandrie & Co (USA), and Ericsson Telecommunications (Singapore).

    In late 2012 he founded Shoffr - a mobile marketing platform for brick and mortar stores in India. With mobile becoming the point of first contact for the Indian populous, Abhishek believes that mobile marketing presents a large opportunity for digital marketers. While a lot is being done to direct online traffic to ecommerce portals, Abhishek believes that there is little innovation being done to convert mobile traffic to offline footfall for physical stores; And in the Indian context, physical stores are here to stay for a very long time. Hence, he set out to solve this problem and launched Shoffr.

    Abhishek welcomes feedback and learns from his mistakes. Surrounded by numbers and data, he is a big proponent of data driven decisions. He will refute any assumption unless it is proven; so much so that the entire Shoffr team has learnt the same gospel. At the moment, Abhishek is busy becoming a growth hacker and goes by the nickname 'Mr. ProveIt'.

    kaustubh gupte
    (Biggest Bhukkad)
    Business Development

    Born in Bombay, brought up in Mumbai, Kaustubh is a home grown Mumbaikar, well acquainted with the city. He moved on to become a start-up recruitment specialist and currently does Business Development at Shoffr. As an all-rounder, he is responsible for generating new customers, handles recruitment, and day-to-day operations of the firm. He has proven to be one of the most efficient resources to the company.

    Kaustubh loves his job, but has various other interests like cricket, food, food, food and food. Rather than pondering about a problem, Kaustubh believes in carving out a solution. Fortunately or unfortunately for every problem he faces, the solution is always the same; Food. He is nicknamed ‘biggest bhukkad’ at office for obvious reasons and currently has no close contender.

    Vivek Varade
    Senior Developer
