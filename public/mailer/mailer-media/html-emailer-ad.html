GullakMaster.com has launched pan India and that too in a new avatar - a mobile web app. In this app you will see 'posters' from your nearest stores promoting their deals. 1. Indicates GullakMaster exclusive deals
2. Display store address
3. Your distance from the store
4. Call the store directly from your mobile
5. View details of the deal
6. Request an offer-code (sent to you via SMS)
7. Validity of an offer
8. Share the deal with your friends
9. Number of deals consumed
With GullakMaster.com you can now request deals at the click of a button - no sign up, no upfront costs, no printouts or paper cuttings.. When you request a deal via the app, an offer-code is sent to your mobile by SMS. To redeem the offer flash the SMS to the store cashier. Its just that simple!
