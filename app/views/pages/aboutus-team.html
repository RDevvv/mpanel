abhishek dadoo
(Mr. ProveIt)
Founder, CEO

Abhishek is a Telecoms & Mobile industry veteran and has worked in Management Consulting, Principal Investments, and Strategy Development. He holds a dual degree in Mathematics & Computer Science from The College of Wooster (USA), MBA from INSEAD, and has worked at PwC (USA), Altman Vilandrie & Co (USA), and Ericsson Telecommunications (Singapore).

In late 2012 he founded GullakMaster - a mobile marketing platform for brick and mortar stores in India. With mobile becoming the point of first contact for the Indian populous, Abhishek believes that mobile marketing presents a large opportunity for digital marketers. While a lot is being done to direct online traffic to ecommerce portals, Abhishek believes that there is little innovation being done to convert mobile traffic to offline footfall for physical stores; And in the Indian context, physical stores are here to stay for a very long time. Hence, he set out to solve this problem and launched GullakMaster.

Abhishek welcomes feedback and learns from his mistakes. Surrounded by numbers and data, he is a big proponent of data driven decisions. He will refute any assumption unless it is proven; so much so that the entire GullakMaster team has learnt the same gospel. At the moment, Abhishek is busy becoming a growth hacker and goes by the nickname 'Mr. ProveIt'.
