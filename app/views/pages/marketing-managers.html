Location Based Targeting

Potential customers are everywhere. And location plays a key role in their buying behaviour. Our research shows that 79% of people will entertain offers if it is in their current neighbourhood. GullakMaster allows customers to type any area or pin-code when searching for discounts. Subsequently, we send discounts from stores closest to the location indicated by the customer.

 

Context Specific Marketing

People desire on the spur of the moment and seek immediate satisfaction. GullakMaster allows customers to SMS any keyword they desire. We match keywords for your brand and send only relevant offers to the customer. This keyword targeting provides instant brand recall to the customer and generates significantly higher conversion rates than other platforms.

 

Mobile Advertising

90% of India is carrying a mobile phone. Unlike other mediums such as TV, News Paper or Bill Boards, a cell phone is a personal device which is not shared with others. This gives us an opportunity to influence individual behaviour by targeting the customer on their mobile phone. Research shows that 98% of SMS are read within one minute. Through SMS, GullakMaster provides you the last mile access to the nearest customer, i.e. moments after customer makes the decision to buy a product or service that you sell.
